Pasta

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into various shapes. It is a staple food in many cultures and is a key ingredient in various dishes such as pasta dishes, soups, and salads. Pasta is often served with sauces, such as tomato sauce, Alfredo sauce, or pesto, and can be topped with a variety of ingredients, such as cheese, vegetables, meats, and herbs. The most common types of pasta include spaghetti, fettuccine, lasagna, macaroni, and ravioli. Pasta is a versatile food that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and is a staple in many diets around the world.
